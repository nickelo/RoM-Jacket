Keymapping

UNIVERSAL FUNCTIONS

HTPC Frontend Map

Select:

A-Button/Start

Go Back:

B-Button

Mouse:

Right Stick

Right Trigger = mouseclick

Left Trigger = r-mousebutton

Navigation:

D-pad/Left Stick

Page-Forward:

Right Bumper

Page-Backward:

Left Bumper

 

Emulator Map

Universal functions of emulators use the same button combination.

POWER-OFF / EXIT:

HOLD Back
HOLD L_Trigger then HOLD R_Stick_Button

SPEED CONTROL:

HOLD Back
then HOLD R_Stick RIGHT to Fast Forward
or HOLD R_Stick LEFT to Slow

REWIND:

HOLD Back
Press L_Thumbstick_Button to enable
then HOLD L_Trigger to Rewind

CAPTURE IMAGE/VIDEO:

HOLD Back
press/hold R_Trigger

QUICKSAVE / LOAD STATE:

HOLD Back
then press D-Pad UP/DOWN to select a slot
press D-Pad LEFT to save a state
press D-Pad RIGHT to load a state

RESET / RESTART:

HOLD Back
HOLD L_Trigger
HOLD L_Stick_Button

MESS/RETROARCH/MAME:

HOLD Back:
press L_Trigger to open the UI

NINTENDO FAMICOM:

HOLD Back
press L_Bumper to eject
DISK press R_Bumper to load DISK

NINTENDO DS:

HOLD Back
press L/R Bumper to adjust layout.
